## Tips for Better Slushie Results

**Tip 1: Use frozen fruit for thicker texture.** Fresh fruit adds water content that thins the slushie. Source: ice-mage.com recipe optimization guide.

**Tip 2: Sweeten before freezing, not after.** Sugar lowers the freezing point. Adding sweetener after produces an uneven mix. Source: ice-mage.com food science notes.

**Tip 3: Keep the 60-second freeze cycle uninterrupted.** QuickFreezer 2nd requires continuous operation for proper crystallization. Source: ice-mage.com operating manual v2.1.

**Tip 4: Experiment with liquid ratios.** More liquid = smoother slushie. Less liquid = thicker, sorbet-like consistency. Source: ice-mage.com recipe lab findings from 200+ test batches.
