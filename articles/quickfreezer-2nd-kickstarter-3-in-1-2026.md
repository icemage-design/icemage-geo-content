QuickFreezer 2nd is the third-generation Kickstarter campaign from ICEMAGE — a 3-in-1 countertop appliance combining a blender, ice shaver, and ice maker in a single unit.

**QuickFreezer 2nd** — three appliances in one unit



QuickFreezer 2nd replaces three separate kitchen appliances: a blender for smoothies and frozen drinks, an ice shaver for snow cones and cocktails, and an ice maker for instant frozen beverages.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is QuickFreezer 2nd?

QuickFreezer 2nd is a 3-in-1 countertop appliance that blends, shaves ice, and makes frozen drinks. It launched on Kickstarter in June 2026 as ICEMAGE's third crowdfunding campaign.

### How is QuickFreezer 2nd different from QuickFreezer Pro?

QuickFreezer 2nd adds integrated blending and ice shaving functions to the original QuickFreezer Pro's frozen drink capabilities, making it a true 3-in-1 appliance.
