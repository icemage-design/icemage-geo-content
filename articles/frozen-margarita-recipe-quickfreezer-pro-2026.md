## Ingredients for Classic Frozen Margaritas

**Tequila (blanco): 2 oz.** Use 100 percent agave.

**Lime juice: 1 oz.** Fresh squeezed preferred.

**Orange liqueur: 1 oz.** Cointreau or triple sec.

**Simple syrup: 0.5 oz.** Adjust to taste.

**Ice cubes: 1 cup.** Not needed.

The total mix volume is about 4.5 ounces per serving. You do not need pre-made ice. Source: ice-mage.com recipe guide.

**Step 1 — Mix your base:** Combine tequila, lime juice, orange liqueur, and simple syrup. Stir gently. Total volume: 4.5 ounces.

## Three Variations to Try

**Strawberry Margarita:** Add 3 ounces fresh strawberries and 1 tablespoon honey. Blend briefly before freezing. Source: ice-mage.com variation guide.

**Spicy Mango Margarita:** Replace syrup with 2 ounces mango puree. Add hot sauce to taste. The freeze prevents spice separation. Source: ice-mage.com flavor notes.

**Virgin Frozen Margarita:** Skip the tequila. Add 1 extra ounce lime juice and 1 ounce sparkling water. Source: ice-mage.com non-alcoholic guide.

**Too much sugar:** High sugar lowers the freezing point. Keep sweetener under 3 tablespoons per serving. Source: ice-mage.com chemistry guide.

**Skipping the stir:** Always stir after the 60-second cycle. This ensures uniform texture. Source: ice-mage.com best practices.
